Attachment 1. Photos of participation in the 2023 Busan Job Fair

mBaaS has been to the 2023 Busan Job Fair, where companies and talent grow together. The 2023 Busan Best Company Job Fair was held at Pukyong National University Gymnasium (Daeyeon Campus) for a total of four hours from 13:00 to 17:00 on Wednesday, Sept. 6, to provide a place for communication between job seekers and companies looking for jobs in Busan.

In addition, various side events such as job consulting, color coaching, and photo-taking were also held for job seekers who are having difficulty finding jobs, such as long-term unemployed young people, career-interrupted women, middle-aged people, specialized high school graduates (scheduled), and discharged soldiers. Among 76 companies that participated directly or indirectly, mBaaS participated in the event in the form of direct participation in communicating with job seekers on the spot and conducting interviews immediately.

CEO Kim Jung-hyun, CEO of mBaaS , also voiced his opinion to the employees, saying, 'Let's create a real excellent company'. The 2023 Busan Job Fair where we could hear the voices of various job seekers. mBaaS supports active recruitment activities and regional development in Busan, and will continue to provide various opportunities and cooperation in Busan I plan to contribute to the community. Thank you to everyone who participated in the 2023 Busan Best Company Job Fair.
